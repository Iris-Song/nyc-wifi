NYC WIFI Spots
About the dataset

The "NYC Wi-Fi Hotspot Locations" dataset is an essential resource for identifying public internet access points across New York City. It details a range of Wi-Fi providers, including CityBridge, LLC, Spot On Networks, AT&T, and partnerships like the Chelsea Improvement Company with Google, offering widespread coverage in various public spaces, neighborhoods like Harlem, and even in select subway stations through Transit Wireless. The dataset also informs users on connectivity methods, such as the New York Public Library's open Wi-Fi network "NYPL," and highlights the public utility aspect of internet access, aiming to bridge digital divides. Notably, it underscores the networks' unsecured nature and the lack of technical support, reflecting the broader initiative to make internet access a universally available public utility, especially crucial for residents and those without regular internet access.
